FAQs

    What is cocktail attire? Here’s what the internet says:

    Cocktail attire is a more relaxed dress code, ideal for daytime weddings or outdoor events. It blends elegance with comfort and is common at many occasions: business events, weddings, baptisms, dinners, openings… It’s generally acceptable to remove your jacket during the event..

    Men (Caballeros)


    You can wear a suit or a combination of dress shirt and trousers with a blazer. Ties or bowties are optional and can be colorful or patterned.

    Women (Damas)


    You can choose a short, midi (knee-length), or long dress, or go for a stylish skirt, blouse, or dressy trousers. Heels are welcome (but not required), and accessories should be tasteful rather than flashy.
